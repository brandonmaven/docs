## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a session">
    Call our API with the payment amount and caller's phone number
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer pays">
    **Voice**: Transfer the call to our IVR â€” we collect card details by phone keypad.
    **SMS**: Customer opens a Stripe Checkout link and pays in the browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get notified">
    We send a webhook with the payment result and (for voice) transfer the call back.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Channels

| Channel | How it works | Best for |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Voice** | Customer calls an IVR number, enters card digits by phone keypad | Mid-call payments during a voice AI conversation |
| **SMS** | Customer receives a Stripe Checkout link | Async payments, post-call follow-up |

## Why Maven?

- **PCI Compliant** - Card data never touches your servers (SAQ A)
- **Two Channels** - Voice IVR or Stripe Checkout link
- **Natural Voice** - ElevenLabs TTS for human-like IVR prompts
- **Transfer Back** - Return customers to your agent after payment
- **Webhooks** - Real-time notifications for payment events
- **Stripe Connect** - Funds go directly to your Stripe account
