Collect payments mid-call in your LiveKit voice agent using Maven.

<Info>
  LiveKit integration is coming soon. [Contact us](mailto:founders@trymaven.com) if you'd like early access.
</Info>

## Overview

1. Your LiveKit agent decides to collect a payment
2. Your server creates a Maven session via the API
3. Transfer the customer to Maven's IVR
4. The customer enters their card details by phone keypad
5. Maven processes the payment and transfers the call back
